Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This document outlines what types of data are collected and stored by CalcuPortal, and how we handle it.

Local Client-Side Calculations

All calculations on CalcuPortal occur client-side within your browser. We do not transmit, collect, or store any numbers, ages, heights, weights, financial figures, or dates that you input into our calculators. Your private calculations remain completely private on your machine.

Log Files & Cookies

Like most websites, we collect basic analytics log files (IP addresses, browser types, referral paths) for system diagnostics. We also use cookies to store theme preferences (such as light/dark mode).
