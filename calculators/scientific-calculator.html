Scientific Calculator Functions

Scientific calculators solve equations relating to geometry, chemistry, physics, and calculus. Standard trigonometry (sin/cos/tan) inputs are evaluated in radians. To compute standard degree values, convert degrees to radians via: Radians = Degrees × (π / 180).
